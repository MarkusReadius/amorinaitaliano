Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially for dinner service. You can book online through our reservations page or call us directly.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer various vegetarian options and can accommodate most dietary restrictions. Please inform your server of any allergies or restrictions.

Do you host private events?

Yes, we have a private dining room available for special events. Please contact us for more information about private dining options and pricing.

Is parking available?

Street parking is available, and there are several parking garages within walking distance. We also offer valet parking during dinner service.
